# Market Intelligence Operating System (MIOS) — overview

MIOS is the always-on layer that makes the platform behave like a research
department: deterministic loops watch markets/government/options/data 24/7;
scheduled multi-agent cycles read everything, challenge themselves, and leave
a synthesized morning read; every interpretation is archived and later graded.

## Daily rhythm
- **Continuous (24/7):** quotes (~30s), bars (6h), options sweeps, news
  (30m), congressional (6h, snapshots), deep history (weekly), score +
  prediction logging (6h), alert engine (~30s).
- **Daily (before login):** intelligence cycle → agent findings + Critic
  challenges + Research Director synthesis. Read it on Research tab → MIOS,
  or ask the AI drawer's `morning` mode (which grounds in the latest cycle).
- **On demand:** ▶ run cycle; any AI drawer mode.

## Morning intelligence answers
What changed overnight (inbox + findings) · what matters most (Director
synthesis) · sectors/companies affected (findings' sector fields + exposure
hits) · government actions (gov agent + Government tab) · narratives
strengthened/weakened (stance history + learning scorecard) · assumptions
changed (scientist agent + assumption monitor) · what to research today
(RESEARCH TODAY section) · what to ignore (IGNORE section — confidence <30).

## Governing philosophy (unchanged)
Evidence changes models. AI organizes evidence. Humans make investment
decisions. Agent output has no code path into models, scores or allocation.
