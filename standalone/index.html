RSI(2) mean-reversion signals — sector ETFs

Validated edge (held out-of-sample). BUY = sector above its 200-day SMA and RSI(2) below 10 — enter near the close. Exit when price closes back above the 5-day SMA (the "Exit 5SMA" column; "to exit" is how far price is from that trigger). Daily signals — act on the close. RSI(2) live is provisional: what RSI(2) would be if today closed at the live price — watch it into the last hour to see a BUY forming before the close. Mean-reversion: works in bull/range markets, vulnerable in crashes (the 200-SMA filter is the guardrail). Not advice; size small.
